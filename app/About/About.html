About

This is a small tool I created to assist myself in making timetabling decisions for university. It is released publicly with no guarantees about its usefulness for you (Please check your timetable against the university website especially if you use the automatic parser. You can either input the options available for a course manually on the Input page, or get the data (closer to) automatically from the UON timetable website. I am aware that this solution is far from ideal, but it is the cleanest way I have been able to do it so far. If you know of a more direct way to get UON timetable data please do let me know.
